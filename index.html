Welcome to NMSM Government College

Neelikkandi Moideen Sahib Memorial (NMSM) Government College, Kalpetta, is a premier public higher education institution located in Vellaramkunnu, near Kalpetta in the Wayanad district of Kerala. Established in 1981 under the auspices of the Government of Kerala, the college operates with a dedicated mission to provide transformative and inclusive university education, particularly serving economically and socially marginalized communities in the region. Affiliated with the University of Calicut, the institution boasts a scenic 25-acre campus and is NAAC-accredited. NMSM Government College offers a diverse academic portfolio, featuring structured four-year undergraduate honors programmes in streams like Computer Science, Chemistry, Development Economics, History, Journalism, and Commerce, alongside specialized postgraduate courses. Known for its strong commitment to women's empowerment, the college maintains a student enrollment with over 80% female representation, continuing its legacy as a pivotal center for academic excellence and social transformation in Wayanad.
