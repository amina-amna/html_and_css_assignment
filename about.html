About Our College

NMSM Government College, Kalpetta is one of the leading government colleges in Wayanad district. The college offers quality higher education in Arts, Science and Commerce streams.

The institution focuses on academic excellence, research, innovation and personality development. Students are encouraged to participate in cultural activities, sports and community service.

Vision

To become a centre of excellence in higher education by providing quality education, ethical values and social responsibility.

Mission

  • Provide quality education.
  • Develop leadership skills.
  • Promote research and innovation.
  • Encourage social commitment.
  • Support holistic student development.
